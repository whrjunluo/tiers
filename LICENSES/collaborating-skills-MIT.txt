Each ships its full MIT license text in its own LICENSE file:

    Source: GuDaStudio/skills
- skills/collaborating-with-gemini  — MIT, Copyright (c) 2025 GudaStudio
    Source: GuDaStudio/skills
- skills/collaborating-with-mimo    — MIT
    Derived from collaborating-with-codex (same structure), adapted for the
    MiMoCode CLI (`mimo run`). Distributed under the same MIT terms.
- skills/collaborating-with-cursor-agent — MIT

    Covered by the repository LICENSE.
- skills/collaborating-with-grok     — MIT
    Original work for this repository (wraps `grok -p --output-format json`).
    Covered by the repository LICENSE.

grok) are covered by the repository LICENSE.
